**C. My remarks to the translation.**  
* a zone -- in Russian slang, a corrective labor colony.  
* the Moon is female gender in Russian.  
* maybe implies muslim drug dealers. maybe not.  
* homosexual prisoners in Russian prisons and colonies are forced by their peers to use a spoon with a hole to mark their lower social standing.  
* not drying -- being perpetually alcohol-intoxicated, a common Russian colloquial euphemism.
